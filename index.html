Back story

Contra is a term used for the game series meaning fighting spirit that spans over a number of years and is hard to keep track of the events but also because time travel is possible in the timeline. Basically though the story follows a team of special forces that serve to protect the galaxy from a variety of threats ranging from Aliens to terrorists. The team is primarily made up of Men,women,robots and aliens. The events in the game take place approximately between the years of 1973 - 4444. The Contra are sent in as the last line of defense to save the earth from alien invaders and the battles are usually against unbelievable odds. The Contra use a variety of special Weaponsand guerila tactics to defeat thier foes.
